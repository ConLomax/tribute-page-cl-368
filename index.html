Robin McLaurin Williams
(July 21, 1951 – August 11, 2014)

Robin Williams was an American actor and comedian known for his improvisational skills and the wide variety of characters he created on the spur of the moment and portrayed on film, in dramas and comedies alike. He sadly passed away in August of 2014, yet his movies continue to inspire and are loved by all.

Robin McLaurin Williams’ roles were extremely varied, but he would often end up being the star of the show on numerous occasions. Below you will find some of his more popular movie roles.

1982: The World According To Garp

1984: The Moscow On The Hudson

1990: Awakenings

1992: Toys, Aladdin

1993: Jumanji

2002: Insomnia, One Hour Photo

2009: World's Greatest Dad

2005: Robots

2006-2014: Night at the Museum Trilogy, Happy Feet, Happy Feet 2
